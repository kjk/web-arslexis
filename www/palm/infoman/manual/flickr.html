Home » Documentation » Flickr upload for InfoMan

InfoMan allows uploading images directly from Treo to your Flickr photo album. To upload a picture:
  • go to Camera application
  • go to pictures view
  • select a picture to upload
  • use "Send..." menu item
  • choose "flickr (InfoMan)" from the list that shows up
  • when used for the first time, you'll have to provide your Flickr account e-mail and password
  • visit your Flickr photo album - the picture should be there
