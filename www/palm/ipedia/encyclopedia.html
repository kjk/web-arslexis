iPedia is a wireless, mobile encyclopedia for Palm and Treo, optimized for Palm smartphones like Treo 600/650.

Requirements: Palm OS device with wireless internet connection (e.g. Treo smartphone).

Please try the FREE TRIAL version first to check compatibility.

iPedia provides the following features:

  • 3 encyclopedias in one: English, German and French
  • currently more than 420 thousand encyclopedia articles (in English encyclopedia) on all imaginable topics
  • articles are constantly updated and their number is growing quickly
  • fast download of encyclopedia articles thanks to optimized network protocol
  • article display optimized for small screens, supporting color and high-resolution screens
  • finding encyclopedia articles based on titles
  • finding encyclopedia articles containing a given word (extended search)
  • copy & paste definition to clipboard for use in other programs
  • support for latest Palm OS 5 devices
  • support for 5-Way Navigator in Treo, Palm Tungsten
