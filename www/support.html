Technical support

If you have problems with our software please first take a look at our extensive documentation. If you can't find answer to your question there, please e-mail us. Please help us by providing as much detail abou the problem as you can, including:

  • which product (name and version) are you using
  • which device are you using
  • describe the problem you're seeing, what happens and what you expect to happen

Discussion group

Join our discussion group. Benefits of participating in discussion group:

  • get notified about product updates
  • get answers to your questions about ArsLexis software
  • provide feedback to the development team
  • suggest improvement ideas for ArsLexis software
  • exchange tips and ideas with other users
