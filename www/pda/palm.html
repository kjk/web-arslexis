ArsLexis offers the following software for Palm OS: iPedia, iNoah, Noah Pro, Thesaurus and Noah Lite.

iPedia

iPedia 1.1 (download free trial version iPedia.prc, 155 kB) is a wireless encyclopedia. iPedia offers 3 encyclopedias:
  • English (more than 390 thousand articles)
  • German (more than 160 thousand articles)
  • French (more than 59 thousand articles)
Articles are constantly updated and their number is growing rapidly, making iPedia essential, up-to-date reference.

iPedia offers quick lookup of articles thanks to optimized network protocol. iPedia supports copy & paste of the definition text, color, high-resolution screens, 5-way navigator and is optimized for Treo 600/650.

iNoah

iNoah 1.1 (download free trial version iNoah.prc, 65 kB) is a wireless English dictionary. iNoah offers 140 thousand words and, unlike all other dictionaries, uses only 65 kB of memory. It supports copy & paste of the definition text, color, high-resolution screens, 5-way navigator and is optimized for Treo.

Noah Pro

Noah Pro 3.0 is an English dictionary. It offers 122 thousand words and thanks to efficient compression the database only takes 5 MB of memory. It supports latest Palm OS devices, high-resolution, color and Treo 5-way navigator support.

Thesaurus

Thesaurus 1.2 is an English thesaurus.

Noah Lite

Noah Lite 1.0 is a free, limited version of Noah Pro.

ArsLexis also offers software for MS Smartphone and Pocket PC.
