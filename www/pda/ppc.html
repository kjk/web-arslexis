ArsLexis offers the following software for Pocket PC PDAs: iNoah, iPedia.

iNoah

iNoah 1.0 (download inoah_ppc_1_0.cab, 71 kB) is a wireless English dictionary. iNoah is a true dictionary on-the-go. It offers definitions of 140 thousand words, uses only 71 kB of memory, color display of definitions and ability to adjust definition font size. It requires Pocket PC device with wireless internet data connection (e.g. Pocket PC-based phones or Pocket PCs with built-in wi-fi or bluetooth connections).

iPedia

iPedia 1.0 (download ipedia_ppc_1_0.cab, 120 kB) is a wireless encyclopedia. With more than 350 thousand articles,it has facts and information about almost anything. The content is constantly being updated and expanded, making iPedia an invaluable, up-to-date, mobile educational resource

ArsLexis also offers software for MS Smartphone and Palm.
