iPedia

iPedia 1.0 (download free trial version iPedia.prc, 155 kB) is a wireless encyclopedia. iPedia offers more than 440 thousand articles (and growing quickly). It offers quick lookup of articles thanks to optimized network protocol. iPedia supports copy & paste of the definition text, color, high-resolution screens, 5-way navigator and is optimized for Treo 600/650.
