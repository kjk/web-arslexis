iPedia is a wireless encyclopedia for Pocket PC.

Requirements: iPedia requires a Pocket PC with wireless internet connection.

Please try the FREE TRIAL version first to check compatibility.

iPedia provides the following features:

  • currently more than 440 thousand encyclopedia articles on all imaginable topics
  • articles are constantly updated and their number is growing quickly
  • fast download of encyclopedia articles thanks to optimized network protocol
  • article display optimized for small screens
  • finding encyclopedia articles based on titles
  • finding encyclopedia articles containing a given word (extended search)
  • user interface optimized for Pocket PC devices
